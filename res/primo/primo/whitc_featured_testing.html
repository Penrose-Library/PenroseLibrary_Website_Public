What is Sherlock?


Sherlock is the new library search tool for finding materials in Penrose Library, Summit libraries, and libraries worldwide.


What can I find in Sherlock?

Sherlock has four different scopes that you can use to limit or expand your search, depending on your needs.

Whitman Library

Use this to search for materials in the Whitman Library catalog, including books, print journals, electronic journals, special collections and audiovisual materials.


Whitman + Summit

Use this to search for and request materials held by Whitman Library and all Summit Libraries.


Whitman + Summit + Articles

Use this to search for content from Whitman, Summit, and journal articles and electronic content from many of Whitman's electronic resources.


Articles

Use this to search for scholarly and popular journal articles from many of Whitman's electronic resources.
